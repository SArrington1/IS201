Commercial Real Estate

The Underwriting Process

  1. Review the OM
    • Note property details (vintage, number of units, architecture,etc...)
    • Assess needed renovations and repairs
    • Evaluate broker's pro forma
  2. Market Research
    • Population growth
    • Historical rent growth
    • Demand drivers
    • Economic drivers
  3. Input T-12 and Rent Roll
  4. Assess potential future renovations
  5. Create a pro forma using assumptions gathered from market data
  6. Input loan details
  7. Back into a desired IRR by changing purchase price
  8. Ensure all metrics are solid
